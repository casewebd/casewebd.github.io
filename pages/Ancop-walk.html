ANCOP Walk


The ANCOP Walk is an annual global initiative organized by ANCOP aimed at generating funds for its diverse programs, which encompass SHELTER, CHILD SPONSORSHIP, and HUMANITARIAN RELIEF. This event exemplifies faith in action. Participants, including individuals and groups, engage in the walk, contributing donations and assisting in fundraising efforts for ANCOP. People of all ages - children, youth, adults, and seniors—come together to advocate for the underprivileged and encourage public support for the cause. Participants in the ANCOP Walk are require to register and pay registration fee. A portion of this fee, specifically $5, is designated to support the St. Vincent de Paul Society's outreach efforts to assist the poor.
