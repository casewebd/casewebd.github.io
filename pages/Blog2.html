ANCOP Walk

The ANCOP Walk is an annual global initiative organized by ANCOP aimed at generating funds for its diverse programs, which encompass SHELTER, CHILD SPONSORSHIP, and HUMANITARIAN RELIEF. This event exemplifies faith in action. Participants, including individuals and groups, engage in the walk, contributing donations and assisting in fundraising efforts for ANCOP. People of all ages—children, youth, adults, and seniors—come together to advocate for the underprivileged and encourage public support for the cause.

Participants in the ANCOP Walk are required to register and pay a registration fee. A portion of this fee, specifically $5, is designated to support the St. Vincent de Paul Society's outreach efforts to assist the poor.

Child Sponsorship Program

The ANCOP Child Sponsorship Program is a one-to-one sponsor-to-scholar connection, providing underprivileged children with the opportunity to build a brighter future. Through this program, each child receives a comprehensive support package that goes beyond education - it encompasses healthcare, socio-cultural enrichment, and spiritual development for the sponsored children and their parents.

Transforming Lives Through Housing

ANCOP Bermuda has been making a profound and lasting impact by providing safe, dignified and sustainable housing for families in need. Over the years, ANCOP Bermuda has successfully constructed 115 homes in the Philippines and other countries, providing countless families with hope and new beginnings.

Learn More
Bringing Hope in Times of Crisis

ANCOP is dedicated to helping those in need, especially during times of disaster and hardship. ANCOP has consistently provided relief to commmunities affected by natural calamities in the Philippines. During the Covid-19 pandemic, ANCOP extended its mission beyond disaster relief by distributing grocery bags in Bermuda to help those who faced financial difficulties due to lockdown and job losses.
