Disaster Relief


Bringing hope in times of crisis

In times of global crises, the charitable organization Answering the Cry of the Poor (ANCOP) Bermuda has consistently reached out to communities in need, both locally and internationally. With unwavering dedication, ANCOP Bermuda has provided significant aid to families affected by natural disasters in the Philippines and has supported local Bermudian families during the COVID-19 pandemic.

**Support for Typhoon Victims in the Philippines**

The Philippines, often hit by powerful typhoons, has found a reliable ally in ANCOP Bermuda. Following Typhoon Haiyan in November 2013, one of the most catastrophic storms on record, ANCOP Bermuda quickly mobilized to assist those in urgent need. Partnering with the Roman Catholic Diocese of Hamilton, the organization raised $65,000 to support relief and recovery efforts in the most affected areas. This initiative was part of a larger community effort in Bermuda, which collectively raised nearly $500,000 for the Philippines' recovery. The funds raised by ANCOP Bermuda were directed towards various essential projects, including the construction of typhoon-resistant homes and the provision of vital supplies to displaced families. These efforts not only met immediate needs but also aided in the long-term recovery of affected communities.

**Assistance During the COVID-19 Pandemic**

The onset of the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020 brought unprecedented challenges globally, and Bermuda was no exception. Aware of the economic difficulties faced by many local families due to lockdowns and job losses, ANCOP Bermuda stepped in to support the local community. The organization distributed over 400 grocery vouchers, each worth $150, to individuals and families severely impacted by the pandemic. This initiative offered much-needed relief to those struggling to meet their basic needs during this challenging time. **A Legacy of Compassion and Solidarity** ANCOP Bermuda's commitment to helping those in need continues to inspire hope and resilience in the communities it serves.
